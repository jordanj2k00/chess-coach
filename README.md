# ♟ Liquid Grave Chess Coach

A personalized chess training app that adapts to your playstyle, teaches your repertoire, and analyzes your games in real time.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🎮 Game Modes

#### 🤖 Play vs Computer
- AI uses minimax (not random)
- Realistic responses to 1.e4:
  - e5
  - c5 (Sicilian)
  - e6 (French)
  - c6 (Caro-Kann)
- AI strength scales with your ELO

---

#### 📚 Opening Theory Practice
- Trains your real repertoire
- Plays opponent moves automatically
- Guides you step-by-step

---

#### 🧠 Training Mode
- Tests your memory of openings
- Must play correct moves
- Gives feedback and explanations

---

#### 🔍 Analysis Board
- Detects opening + variation
- Shows best move
- Displays evaluation

---

## 🧠 Coaching System

### 💡 Move Explanations
Example:  
Play d4  
Why: Strike the center and open lines

---

### 📊 Evaluation Bar
- Shows who is winning
- Updates live during game

---

### 🎯 Adaptive Coach
Tracks your habits:
- Early queen moves
- Over-aggression
- Pawn pushing

---

### ♟ Position Plans
Recognizes:
- Open center
- French structure
- Kingside attack

---

## 🎮 Controls

- Flip Board → switch perspective  
- Resign → end game  
- Reset → restart  
- Show Best Move → highlights strongest move  

---

## 📈 ELO System

- Starts at 1200  
- Updates after each game  
- AI adjusts to your level  

---

## 🤖 AI

- Uses minimax algorithm  
- Makes realistic decisions  
- Occasionally makes mistakes like a human  

---

## 🛠 How to Run

Open `index.html` in your browser

---

## 🧠 Vision

This is a personal chess coach that:
- Learns how you play  
- Trains your openings  
- Explains your moves  
- Helps you improve
